Game Info

  1. Normal Mode and Random Mode: You have 3 chances.

  2. Score Mode: You have 1 chance.

  3. The score will be displayed only in Score Mode.

  4. You can combine Score Mode with Normal or Random Mode for added challenge.

  5. Left-click your mouse to start the game.

  6. Move your mouse to control the paddle's position.

  7. Break as many bricks as possible to achieve the highest score.

  8. Be careful not to miss the ball to avoid losing chances.

  9. Aim for combos to maximize your score.

  10. Your skill and strategy will determine your success.
